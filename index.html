It seems as though the world is collectively turning both its eyes and hearts towards the situation of Palestians in Gaza. While malnourishment is plauging such a large proportion of the population, we are reminded that God upholds the cause of the oppressed and marginalised.

God is just, none can escape his pure, righteous judgement. In Jesus Christ we have an advocate, such that through faith in Him and His resurrection, we may be holy and blameless in His sight.

Let us thank God for the gifts he has given us, for he is most merciful and his gifts are plentiful. One of them is food. We recieve this gift with thanksgiving, and take this oppurtunity to explore and celebrate the culture of the Palestinian people, whom God created. We trust in the mercy and goodness of God during these dark hours.


Amen.
